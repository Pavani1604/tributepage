Tejaswini Mangona

About her accomplishments

  • Tejaswini Mangona was born on 19 may 1994, she is an indian model and beauty pageant holder.

  • A medical doctor by profession, she won title of Divine Miss Earth India 2019.

  • Mangona represented india at Miss Earth 2019 where she won Miss Global Choice and Eco Trivia awards.

  • She was a member of National cadet Corps and awarded as Indias's Best NCC cadet and Best Shooter among 1.3 millions of cadets.

  • She was conferred with the Young Acheiver Award at South Asian Association of Regional Cooperationin srilanka.

  • Mangona is also trained in Bharatnatayam, a major form of indian classical dance.

  • Mangona holds a diploma in yoga training and has worked as yoga instructor in hyderabad.
